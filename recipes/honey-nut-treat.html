Honey Nut Treat-The Elder Scrolls V: Skyrim

Sweet balls made of nuts and honey

Woah weary traveler, thanks for walking into my inn. I have plenty of food and drink in stock if you fancy yourself an ale or if you'd rather I can whip you up some of my famous Honey Nut Treats. This treat is nuts in various forms, honey, and simple cereals for any traveler to take a break with. Simple, nutritious and tasty.

Ingredients

  • 40g (about a handful) mixture of nuts (walnuts, haelnuts, cashews)
  • 30g peeled unsalted peanuts
  • 60g dry oatmeal
  • 3 tbsps multiflower honey
  • 3 tbsps peanut butter

Steps

  1. Cut nuts roughly into smaller pieces â€“ walnuts into quarters, hazelnuts and cashews in half.
  2. Add honey and peanut butter to the saucepan. Heat on low power for about 1 minute, stirring constantly. When the mass becomes smooth and combined, turn off the heater.
  3. Put nuts, peeled peanuts and oatmeal into a warm honey mass. Mix everything thoroughly. When the mixture has cooled down a little, put it under the cover for 30 minutes in the fridge.
  4. Take mass out of the fridge and form balls of various sizes, which should be put on sticks. Chill them in the fridge.
  5. Serve hot with an additional potion of butter and powdered sugar.
  6. Remove Honey Nut Treat from fridge about 30 minutes before serving. It goes well with a mug of dark,bitter beer.
